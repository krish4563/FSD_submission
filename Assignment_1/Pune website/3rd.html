Welcome to Pune

Pune, known as the cultural capital of Maharashtra, is a vibrant city with a rich history and a modern outlook. It is famous for its educational institutions, historical landmarks, and scenic beauty.

Top Attractions

Shaniwar Wada

Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification in the city of Pune. Built in 1732, it was the seat of the Peshwas of the Maratha Empire until 1818.

Aga Khan Palace

Aga Khan Palace is a majestic building that was built in 1892 by Sultan Aga Khan III. It is one of the most important landmarks in Indian history.

Parvati Hill

Parvati Hill is a picturesque hillock that offers a panoramic view of Pune city. It is home to the Parvati Temple and Peshwa Museum.

Cultural Heritage

Pune is known for its cultural heritage. It hosts numerous cultural events, classical music concerts, and traditional dance performances throughout the year.

Local Cuisine

Pune offers a delectable array of Maharashtrian cuisine. From spicy Misal Pav to the sweet Puran Poli, the city has something to satisfy every palate.
